About us

Why should you consider a career with us? What makes Hot Beans Web the right place for you?

Who we are

We design and build websites with purpose. Every choice is made with the user and goal in mind, whether that’s improving accessibility, simplifying content, or just making things look and feel better. The focus is always on clean layouts, and smooth performance across all devices.

What we do

We're a small, dedicated team committed to doing things right. We pay attention to detail, stay flexible, and keep the bigger picture in mind. Our work combines technical skill and creative thinking, and we enjoy finding simple, effective solutions through design and development.

Why us

We create thoughtful, effective designs that are clear, accessible, and consistent. Studies show 94% of first impressions are design-related, and visitors form opinions within just 50 milliseconds. We focus on functionality as much as appearance.

Our Process

Our process is simple and straightforward. We start by understanding what’s needed, then plan and design before moving into development. We test throughout, check everything across devices, and stay open to feedback. Even after launch, we stay involved to ensure everything runs smoothly.
